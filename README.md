This project analyzes customer churn using the Telco Customer Churn dataset to identify the key factors influencing customer attrition.
The analysis combines traditional business intelligence techniques with AI-assisted features in Power BI to uncover drivers of churn and communicate insights effectively.

The goal is to help stakeholders understand why customers churn and identify actionable retention strategies.

The key business questions addressed in this project are:

How do services, payment methods, and customer support affect churn?

ğŸ” Key Insights

Month-to-month contracts have the highest churn rate, indicating low customer commitment.

Customers using fiber optic internet services show significantly higher churn.

Electronic check payment method is associated with higher churn compared to other methods.

Customers without tech support churn more than those with support.

Customers with partners or dependents tend to churn less.
